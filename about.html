Sabrina's Background

Sabrina was born in Pennsylvania on May 11th, 1999. She has pursued music since she was young, but she really bolstered her way into fame after landing the role of Maya Hart on hit Disney show Girl Meets World. She released her first album, EVOLution in October of 2016. Her popularity has only increased since then. Her next title album, Singuar, comes out Novmember 11th, 2018.
